About
Hello! I am Mitchell Baertlein, a student at Oregon State University studying Electrical and Computer Engineering. I grew up in Tillamook, Oregon (about an hour and a half west of Portland) and am still faintly in love with the ocean. I have worked with Hampton Lumber for the last two summers, the first time under Electricians and the second time as a Controls Engineering apprentice. They were both really great experiences and I feel very lucky to have happened upon them. Right now I have a big interest in either prosthetics or power generation, and while I haven't really done any big project related to these topics, I hope to someday get a chance to at least work on these types of things.
